About Gold Coast

Gold Coast is a coastal city located in Queensland, Australia. Situated on the country's east coast, it is renowned for its stunning beaches, sunny climate, and vibrant entertainment scene. With a population of over 600,000 people, Gold Coast is a popular tourist destination known for its theme parks, surfing spots, and bustling nightlife.

Things to Do

  • Soak up the sun on Surfers Paradise Beach
  • Experience thrilling rides and attractions at theme parks like Dreamworld and Warner Bros. Movie World
  • Explore the natural beauty of Lamington National Park
  • Indulge in shopping and dining at Broadbeach or Marina Mirage
  • Take a leisurely stroll along the beachfront promenade of The Spit
