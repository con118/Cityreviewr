About Hobart

Hobart is the capital city of Tasmania, an island state in Australia. It is located in the southeast part of the state and is known for its historic charm, picturesque waterfront, and natural beauty. With a population of over 200,000 people, Hobart offers a unique blend of culture, outdoor activities, and culinary delights.

Things to Do

  • Explore the historic Salamanca Place and its vibrant market
  • Visit the Museum of Old and New Art (MONA)
  • Take a stroll through the Royal Tasmanian Botanical Gardens
  • Enjoy a scenic drive up to Mount Wellington
  • Embark on a cruise to explore the stunning waterways of Bruny Island
