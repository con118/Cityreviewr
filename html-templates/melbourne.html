About Melbourne

Melbourne is the largest city in Australia, located on the southeast coast of the country. It is known for its vibrant arts scene, diverse culinary offerings, and sporting events. The city has a population of over 5 million people and is a major cultural, economic, and sporting hub in Australia.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Federation Square
  • Explore the Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG)
  • Stroll along the Yarra River
  • Discover street art in Hosier Lane
  • Experience the Queen Victoria Market
