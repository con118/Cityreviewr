About Darwin

Darwin is the capital city of the Northern Territory in Australia. Located on the northern coast, it is known for its tropical climate, rich Indigenous culture, and unique natural wonders. With a population of over 150,000 people, Darwin offers a laid-back atmosphere and serves as a gateway to the stunning landscapes of the Top End.

Things to Do

  • Explore the colorful Mindil Beach Sunset Market
  • Visit the fascinating Museum and Art Gallery of the Northern Territory
  • Take a cruise along the stunning Darwin Harbour
  • Experience the vibrant energy of the Darwin Waterfront Precinct
  • Embark on an adventure to the nearby Kakadu National Park
